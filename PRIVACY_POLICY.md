# Privacy Policy for SpendWise

**Effective Date: March 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the SpendWise mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created as an open-source, ad-free service. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use "AS IS".

### Data Collection and Use
The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or financial data on any external servers. 

All data entered into the Application (including expenses, income, categories, and budgets) is stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database (Android Room). You have complete control over your data.

### Third-Party Services
The Application does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. There are no tracking scripts, analytics, or advertisements embedded in the Application.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy
I may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
